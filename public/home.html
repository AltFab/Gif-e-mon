How to play Gif-E-Mon!

OBJECTIVE

Oh no! Evil gifs are trying to take over the internet! You must use your gif to defend the internet from total domination!

BUTTONS

Attack - use this button to attack your enemy Gif for attack power

Defense - use this button to defend yourself from the enemy

Potion - use this button to restore your gif's HP level

SCORE SYSTEM

1. Attack enemy
-increase score based on the loss of HP from the enemy gif
2. Kills enemy
- increase points by a static 20 points for each kill
3. Use none of your potions
- each round user remains with 3 potions give bonus points 5 points.
- if user has less than 3 potions no bonus points
